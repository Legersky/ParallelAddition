\textbf{ Eisenstein\_2-block }

The elements $ \left2\omega - 1, 2\omega, \omega + 1, -\omega - 1, -2\omega, -2\omega + 1\right \in \mathcal{B}$ have no representative  modulo $\beta-1$ in the alphabet $\mathcal{A}$.
